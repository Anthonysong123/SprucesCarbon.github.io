Renewable energy D-MRV

Region: Southeast Asia (partner markets)

Pilot infrastructure for distributed solar digital verification—demonstrating how local governments can achieve digital green infrastructure achievements while proving Spruces’ technical ability to account for renewable generation data with precision and defensibility.

Objectives

  • Establish monitored distributed solar digital verification across partner markets
  • Support government digital green infrastructure and transparency goals
  • Prepare cryptographic, audit-ready evidence for independent methodology review

No registered credits, no issued tonnes, and no named government endorsement are implied by this programme description.

Roadmap

Smart Clean Cookstove Initiative

Status: Coming soon / future ecosystem

We will seamlessly extend our mature D-MRV cryptographic anti-fraud foundation to community clean energy infrastructure in remote rural Southeast Asia. By precisely monitoring the physical operation of smart gasification devices, we help assess contributions to reduced local deforestation while supporting development of clean cooking emission reduction projects with high social and health premiums that meet international digital standards.

Programme design, partners, and standard selection remain subject to consultation.

One measurement architecture, multiple asset classes

Spruces’ core intellectual property is the digital asset origination protocol—a service architecture that extends across asset classes. The same governance layers are intended to extend to additional scenarios, including micro-grids and island/off-grid renewables, without rebuilding trust infrastructure from scratch.
