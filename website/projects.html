In development — pilot

Distributed Solar D-MRV Initiative

Region: Southeast Asia (partner markets)

Building a monitored network for distributed photovoltaic assets in challenging terrain and grid conditions—translating generation into structured emission-reduction evidence.

Objectives

  • Improve measurement continuity for dispersed assets
  • Support government and developer transparency goals
  • Prepare evidence packages for independent methodology review

No registered credits, no issued tonnes, and no named government endorsement are implied by this programme description.

Roadmap

Community clean cooking & device monitoring

Planned extension of the same monitoring and verification service model to community clean-cooking programmes—targeting methodologies with strong social co-benefits, pending feasibility and validation.

Programme design, partners, and standard selection remain subject to consultation.

One measurement architecture, multiple asset classes

Spruces’ core intellectual property is the digital asset origination protocol—a service architecture that extends across asset classes. The same governance layers are intended to extend to additional scenarios, including micro-grids and island/off-grid renewables, without rebuilding trust infrastructure from scratch.
